    How to play

    Rain tiles onto the lake and coax all eight blossoms to bloom.

    • 🁢Place tiles. Drag your tile onto a glowing space beside the lake. Edges must match their neighbors.
    • 🔄Rotate. Tap your hand tile (or press R / Space) to turn it.
    • 🎴Shuffle. Tap the deck (or press D) to swap your tile for another.
    • 🌸Bloom. Complete matching patterns to open blossoms. All eight blossoms win the round.
    • 💧Play in the pond. Tap the water for ripples, nudge lily pads, and say hi to the koi.
